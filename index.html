The release of Squane

After two years of inactivity, we have finally published the game Squane. We rebuilt it with the Godot's engine and added some new features.
The game has got 6 five-star reviews in two days... Who knows what the future holds for it. Squane is available for free on Google Play to phones with android 2.3 and up.

Godot's game jam

We have been participating in a game jam which's theme was "two buttons". The game engine used was Godot.
We performed quite well. We got the 5th place out of 41 possible.

All the games were rated by developers and other Itch.io users.
The rating is made up of 4 smaller ones:
Overall: 3.917(stars)
Graphics and sounds: 4.000(stars)
Theme("Two Buttons"): 4.417(stars)
Gameplay: 3.333(stars)

Our game is called "The umbrella".
