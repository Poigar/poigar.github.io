About us

Who we are?

We are a group of 3 members, who make games in our free time.

Our names: Reinards, Niks, Emīls.

There are some games which we made together, and there are some games made by individual members.
We have been making games since 2015.
Emīls is our newest member.

What do we do?

As mentioned above, we make games in our free time.
We have one browser-based game called Gatito War, one PC, Linux, MacOS game called The Umbrella and a mobile game called Squane.

All the games are free to play, Squane is published on Google Play.
